Lunar AI

Lunar AI transforms the way households manage power by combining artificial intelligence with smart solar technology. Acting as your home’s energy command center, it analyzes real-time data from weather forecasts, solar generation, and device activity to optimize every watt of electricity. From predicting sunlight availability to distributing power intelligently between appliances and storage, Lunar AI ensures peak efficiency without sacrificing comfort. It learns your patterns, adapts to environmental changes, and continuously fine-tunes usage to cut costs and reduce carbon impact. Designed with a modern, minimal interface, Lunar AI makes sustainable living not just possible, but beautifully simple.
