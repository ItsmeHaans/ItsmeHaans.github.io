Lunar AI

Lunar AI is a sleek web platform that automatically manages your electronic usage and solar battery saving power, money, and the planet, all at once. Designed for modern households, Lunar AI acts as the intelligent brain of your home’s energy ecosystem. It analyzes real-time data from solar panels, weather forecasts, and consumption patterns to optimize when and how your devices use electricity. From now till then, Lunar AI continuously learns your habits, balances energy flows, and adapts to changing conditions — ensuring every watt of sunlight is used wisely. Whether it’s charging your EV when solar output peaks or reducing appliance load during cloudy hours, Lunar AI handles it all seamlessly. Built with cutting-edge AI algorithms and an elegant user dashboard, Lunar AI turns sustainable living into an effortless experience. You don’t just monitor your energy you master it.
